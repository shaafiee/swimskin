🠛 Swim.skin (SSK) 🠛
Swim.skin - SSK

Swim.skin is a community for innovating connections between blockchain technologies and the world of fashion. We will collectively support and promote other kindred NFT projects on fashion.

We can join this community by simply minting a Swim.skin NFT. There are a total of 7,777 unique NFTs depicting original swimwear designs. Every wallet can own no more than 5 tokens at a time.

We get an exclusive portal, only available to holders of Swim.skin tokens, where they can discuss projects, share ideas and find new ways to have lots of fun. Using this portal we can also collectively decide the future of our community.

🠛 Traits 🠛
Traits

Swim.skin is a collection of original hand-drawn artwork. Every token is generated using over 200 traits across more than a dozen categories. The permutation of this many traits across their categories make for 7,777 unique permutations.

Amongst the 7,777 Swim.skin NFTs are 9 unique swimwear designs for us to hunt down.

🠛 Roadmap 🠛
Roadmap

We get access to the Swim.skin community portal the moment we mint a token. We will enjoy the rest of the benefits as the community grows.

Appreciating that this is a bear market, we are looking forward to a slow mint. So, we will be basing our roadmap on revenue rather than the sell-through rate.

Minting phase

50 Put 10 into a raffle for 5 lucky holders of Swim.skin.
100 Put 20 into a raffle for 10 lucky holders of Swim.skin.
155 Swim.skin Coin (SSKC) virtual currency will be launched with a starting liquidity pool of 40. A quarter of the SSKC supply will be fairly airdropped to Swim.skin token holders.

Post-mint phase

For every 3000 of secondary sales volume mark reached, 50 will go into the Swim.skin liquidity pool.
For every 6000 of secondary sales volume mark reached, 150 will be spent on holding an event. The type of event and its location will be decided by the Swim.skin community.
Launch MVP of the Protoskin Fashion Design Tool within 3 months of minting all 7,777 SSK tokens. Only holders will have access to this MVP.
Launch the Protoskin Fashion Design System within the 12 months after launching the MVP. Holders will have full access to this cloud system, whilst the rest will need to pay a membership fee.
